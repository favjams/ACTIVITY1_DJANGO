About Glamour Cosmetics

Our Story

Glamour Cosmetics was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to empower individuals to express their unique beauty through high-quality, cruelty-free cosmetics. Our journey began in a small studio in New York City, where our founder, Sarah Johnson, started experimenting with natural ingredients to create makeup that was both effective and kind to the skin.

Over the years, we've grown from a local favorite to a global brand, but our core values remain the same. We believe that beauty comes in all shapes, sizes, and colors, and our products are designed to enhance, not mask, your natural beauty.

Today, Glamour Cosmetics offers a wide range of makeup and skincare products, all created with the same passion and attention to detail that inspired our first formulations. We're committed to using only the finest ingredients, ethical production methods, and sustainable packaging to bring you products that you can feel good about using.

Our Values

Cruelty-Free

We never test on animals and only partner with cruelty-free suppliers.

Natural Ingredients

We prioritize natural, skin-loving ingredients in all our formulations.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices.
